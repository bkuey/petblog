My Dog Rocky

Rocky was my first and only family dog that we ever had. We got him when we was a puppy and he was the most energetic dog I've ever met. We had him for about 5 years and we had to give him away because we were moving and it was my biggest heartbreak. I hope he continued to live a wonderful dog life.

Here are some of the things he liked to do:

  • Chase and Race
  • Cuddle
  • Hurdle over rocks
  • Follow me around
